Everything we aspire to do

GebereNet is an emerging platform designed to support Ethiopia’s agricultural community. It connects farmers, organizations, and businesses working in the agriculture sector by offering space to collaborate, share knowledge, and grow together. The platform highlights the importance of partnerships across all levels—local growers to international stakeholders.

🌍 Vision

To cultivate a resilient, inclusive, and collaborative agricultural ecosystem in Ethiopia, empowering farmers and stakeholders to drive sustainable development through shared knowledge and innovation.

🎯 Mission

GebereNet connects Ethiopia’s agricultural community by facilitating access to resources, partnerships, and digital tools. We champion collaborative growth, market access, and evidence-based practices that transform livelihoods and unlock local and global opportunities.

📌 Objectives

  • Strengthen local capacity and technical knowledge across agricultural sectors.
  • Enable seamless collaboration among farmers, businesses, and development organizations.
  • Provide access to data, markets, and policy dialogue.
  • Promote evidence-based solutions tailored to Ethiopia’s diverse agroecological contexts.

🔗 Core Values

  • Inclusivity: Every voice matters—from smallholder farmers to international partners.
